Hobe Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

Hobe is a short-form video platform where viewers tip creators using Mobile Money, operated from Kigali, Rwanda. Contact: muyombanohappy@gmail.com.

What we collect

Your phone number (used as your account identity and Mobile Money payout destination), your display name and handle, videos you upload, and records of tips, purchases and withdrawals you make. We log basic technical data (device type, app version) to keep the service working.

What we do with it

We use your phone number to sign you in and to send your money to the right wallet. We keep a permanent record of every financial transaction, as financial regulation requires. We do not sell personal data, and we do not show third-party advertising.

Payments

Payments run over MTN Mobile Money and Airtel Money. When you pay or get paid, the operator processes your phone number and the amount under their own terms and privacy policies. We never see or store a PIN.

Your content

Videos you upload are public. You can request removal of your videos or your account by contacting us; financial records are retained as required by law even after account deletion.

Reporting and moderation

Anyone can report a video in the app. Reported content is reviewed and removed if it breaks our rules. We may suspend accounts involved in fraud or abuse.

Data location and security

Data is stored on servers we control, with payment credentials kept separate from application data. Money balances are derived from a tamper-evident transaction ledger.

Children

Hobe is not directed at children under 13, and Mobile Money accounts require the operator's own age verification.

Changes

We will post any changes to this policy on this page.
